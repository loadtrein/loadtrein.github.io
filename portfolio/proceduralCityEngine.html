Procedural City Engine

« Back to portfolio

Nowadays lots of games and blockbuster films make use of virtual cities as one of their most common resources. This project was developed to be an engine that procedurally generated Manhattan-like cities ready to be used within a project.

By modifying different parameters and the use of some predefined hotkeys, the user is able to determine the dimensions of the city and let the engine create it automatically, toggle the render of the different elements that belong to the city or just select a virtual walk-through tour around it.


This are its main characteristics:

  • City and Buildings layout generated by using Binary Space partitioning
  • Mitre joint technique used in the intersection of the roads
  • Roads mapped over the orography of the terrain
  • Different 3D models procedurally placed to obtain a realistic output
  • Automatic Walk-through mode to offer a virtual visit of the city
  • Developed in C++ and OpenGL
